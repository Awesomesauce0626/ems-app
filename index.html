Privacy Policy – One Tap EMS App

Effective Date: [Insert Date]

1. Information We Collect

  • Location Data: GPS coordinates or manual location details during emergencies.
  • Emergency Details: Incident type, description, and number of victims.
  • Device Information: Basic device identifiers for compatibility and performance.
  • User Account Data: Names, roles, and contact numbers for EMS personnel and admins.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Coordinate rapid emergency response.
  • Track responders’ live locations while “On Duty.”
  • Generate official reports and analytics for provincial safety planning.

3. Data Sharing

  • Shared only with authorized EMS personnel, dispatchers, and administrators.
  • SMS gateway providers may process alerts in low-connectivity areas.
  • No data is sold or shared with advertisers or unrelated third parties.

4. Data Retention & Deletion

  • Emergency records archived securely; administrators may delete outdated or incorrect records.
  • User accounts can be deleted upon request or role termination.

5. Security

We use encrypted communication channels, secure cloud infrastructure, and role-based access controls to protect sensitive data.

6. User Rights

  • Citizens may report emergencies anonymously.
  • EMS personnel and admins may request account deletion or correction.
  • All users have the right to transparency regarding data collection and usage.
